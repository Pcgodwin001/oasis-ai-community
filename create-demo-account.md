## Demo Scenario: "Crisis in 8 Days"

### The Story
Sarah is a single mother working part-time at a local grocery store. She struggles to make ends meet and is currently $94 short for rent due on Nov 12. The app helps her:

1. **Predict the Crisis:** Dashboard shows she'll be short in 8 days
2. **Find Solutions:** ZENO suggests 3 actionable solutions
3. **Discover Missing Benefits:** She's eligible for $411/month in WIC but never applied
4. **Locate Resources:** Map shows 3 food banks within 2 miles
5. **Optimize Spending:** Receipt analysis shows she can save $87/month by switching stores

### Expected Demo Flow

1. **Login** → Shows crisis warning immediately
2. **Dashboard** → Financial health score: 62/100, Crisis in 8 days

5. **ZENO Response:**

6. **Eligibility Checker** → Discovers $411/month in missing WIC benefits
7. **Resource Map** → Shows nearby food banks to preserve EBT balance
8. **Result:** Sarah can avoid crisis + gain $411/month permanently
